What Is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a digital tool designed to calculate a person’s age from their date of birth to the current date or any selected date. Using a date of birth age calculator, you can determine:

  • Your exact age in years, months, and days
  • The number of months or days since birth
  • The difference between two dates
  • Your age on a specific future or past date
