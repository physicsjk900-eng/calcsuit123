What Is a Scientific Calculator?

A scientific calculator is a specialized calculator designed to handle complex mathematical functions. Unlike a basic calculator that performs simple operations like addition and subtraction, a scientific math calculator includes advanced functions used in mathematics, physics, engineering, and statistics.

Common functions available in a scientific calculator online include:

  • Trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan)
  • Logarithmic calculations (log, ln)
  • Exponential functions
  • Square roots and powers
  • Parentheses and complex equations
  • Constants and advanced operations
