What Is an Energy Converter?

An energy converter is a digital tool that converts a value from one unit of energy to another. It supports a wide range of commonly used energy units, including:

  • Joules (J) – SI unit of energy
  • Calories (cal) – Nutritional energy unit
  • Kilowatt-hours (kWh) – Electrical energy unit
  • British Thermal Units (BTU) – Imperial energy unit

With a free energy converter online, you can easily switch between units without manual calculations, ensuring precise results for scientific, industrial, or everyday use.
