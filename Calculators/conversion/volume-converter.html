What Is a Volume Converter?

A volume converter is a digital tool that converts a value from one unit of volume to another. It supports a wide range of units, including:

  • Metric units: liters (L), milliliters (mL), cubic meters (m³)
  • Imperial units: gallons (UK & US), quarts, pints, fluid ounces
  • Common cooking units: cups, tablespoons, teaspoons

With a free volume converter online, you can easily switch between units without manual calculations, making it reliable for both professional and everyday use.
