What Is a Weight Converter?

A weight converter is a digital tool designed to convert a value from one unit of weight to another. It supports a wide range of units including:

  • Metric units: milligrams (mg), grams (g), kilograms (kg), tonnes (t)
  • Imperial units: ounces (oz), pounds (lb), stones (st)
  • US Customary units: pounds, ounces

With a weight converter online, you can easily switch between units without manual calculations, ensuring accuracy in all situations.
