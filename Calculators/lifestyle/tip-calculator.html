What Is a Tip Calculator?

A tip calculator is a digital tool that calculates the gratuity amount based on the total bill and desired tip percentage. It can also divide the total cost among multiple people for fair bill splitting.

  • Total bill amount input
  • Tip percentage selection (customizable)
  • Number of people to split the bill
  • Instant calculation of tip per person and total per person

With a free tip calculator online, you can ensure accurate tipping and fair sharing of costs without manual math.
