What Is an Average Calculator?

An average calculator is a digital tool designed to perform basic statistical calculations for a set of numbers. It simplifies tasks such as finding:

  • Mean (Arithmetic Average) – The sum of all numbers divided by the count.
  • Median – The middle number in a sorted data set.
  • Sum – The total of all numbers in the set.
  • Count – The number of entries in the data set.
