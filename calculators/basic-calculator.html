Mastering the Basics

The basic calculator is the cornerstone of modern mathematics, designed to handle the four fundamental operations: **addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division**.

Order of Operations

Always remember the **PEMDAS** rule (Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplication & Division from left to right, Addition & Subtraction from left to right). While this basic tool handles one operation at a time, keeping this logic in mind ensures mathematical accuracy in your complex projects.

Fun Fact

The first mechanical calculator, the Pascaline, was invented by Blaise Pascal in 1642. It could only add and subtract, yet it paved the way for the digital age we live in today.
