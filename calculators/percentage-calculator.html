Percentage Calculator – Calculate X% of Y Easily

🧠 1. Basic Concept of Percentage
A percentage (%) means “per hundred.” $X\% = \frac{X}{100}$. So, calculating X% of Y means: $X\% \text{ of } Y = \frac{X}{100} \times Y$.


🔢 2. Percentage Formula

$$X\% \text{ of } Y = \frac{X \times Y}{100}$$

Using this math formula, you can find the percentage value of any number quickly and accurately.


✏️ 3. Step-by-Step Method

Example: Find 20% of 150

  • Step 1: Convert percentage into fraction ($20\% = \frac{20}{100}$)
  • Step 2: Multiply by Y ($\frac{20}{100} \times 150$)
  • Step 3: Solve to get the result: 30

⚡ 4. Shortcut Method

Instead of full calculation, convert % into decimal:

10% = 0.10

25% = 0.25

50% = 0.50
