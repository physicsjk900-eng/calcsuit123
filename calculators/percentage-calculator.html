Understanding Percentages

A **percentage (%)** literally means "per hundred." It is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100.

The Universal Formula

$$ \text{Result} = \frac{\text{Percentage}}{100} \times \text{Whole} $$

Pro Tip: Retail Math

To find a 20% discount quickly in your head, divide the price by 10 and then multiply by 2.

$150 / 10 = $15
$15 * 2 = $30 (Discount)
$150 - $30 = $120 (Final Price)
