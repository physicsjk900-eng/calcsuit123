What Is an Area Converter?

An area converter is a digital tool that converts a value from one unit of area to another. It supports a wide range of units, including:

  • Metric units: square meters (m²), square kilometers (km²), hectares (ha)
  • Imperial units: square feet (ft²), square yards (yd²), acres (ac)
  • US Customary units: square miles, acres, square feet

With a free area converter online, you can switch between units effortlessly without manual calculations.
