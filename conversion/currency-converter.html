Understanding Foreign Exchange

"Forex (FX) markets are the largest in the world, with over $7.5 trillion traded daily. Exchange rates fluctuate based on interest rates, economic stability, and geopolitical events. When traveling, remember that 'Market Rate' is different from 'Retail Rate' — banks usually add a 1% to 3% fee on top of the rates you see here."

Pro Travel Tip

Always pay in the **local currency** on card terminals abroad. Let your own bank handle the conversion — they almost always offer a better rate than the merchant's payment processor.
