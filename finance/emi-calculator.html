What Is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment you make to repay a loan over a specified tenure. It includes:

  • Principal amount – The original loan amount
  • Interest – Charged by the lender on the outstanding loan

$$EMI = [P \times R \times (1+R)^n] \div [(1+R)^n – 1]$$
