What Is an Interest Calculator?

An interest calculator is a digital tool that automatically computes the interest earned or paid on a principal amount. It supports two main types:

Simple Interest (SI)

Calculated only on the principal amount:

SI = P × R × T ÷ 100

Compound Interest (CI)

Calculated on principal and accumulated interest:

CI = P × (1 + R ÷ n)nt – P
