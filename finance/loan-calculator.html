Borrower FAQ

What is a Reverse Loan Calculation?

It's the process of finding a missing loan parameter (like the true interest rate) when you already know the Monthly EMI and other terms. It helps verify if a lender's stated rate is accurate.

Should I pay monthly or yearly?

Monthly extra payments generally save more interest than waiting until the end of the year to pay a lump sum, because the principal is reduced every month, lowering the interest charge for following months.
