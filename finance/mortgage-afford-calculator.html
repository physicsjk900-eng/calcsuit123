What Is a Mortgage Affordability Calculator?

A mortgage affordability calculator estimates the maximum home price you can afford based on your financial situation. It considers key factors like:

  • Income: Salary, bonuses, or other sources of income
  • Monthly debts: Loans, credit card payments, or other obligations
  • Down payment: Savings available for your home purchase
  • Interest rates: Current mortgage rates for accuracy
  • Loan term: Duration of your mortgage

With a free mortgage affordability calculator online, you can quickly see a realistic estimate of the homes you can afford without overextending your budget.
