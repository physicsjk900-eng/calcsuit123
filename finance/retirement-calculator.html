The 4% Rule Explained

Our FIRE Engine utilizes the Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR) logic established by the Trinity Study. By dividing your annual expenses by your SWR (typically 4%), you arrive at the investment total required to sustain your lifestyle indefinitely without depleting the original principal.
