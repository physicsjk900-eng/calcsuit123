BMI Calculator – Professional Online Tool

A BMI calculator (Body Mass Index) is a universally recognized screening tool used by healthcare professionals and individuals to quickly assess if a person's body weight is appropriate for their height.

Our free online BMI calculator provides instant classification into clinical categories including Underweight, Normal, Overweight, and Obese, helping you monitor your health goals with precision.

The Mathematical formula

BMI = Weight (kg) / [Height (m)]²

BMI is a proxy for body fatness. While it doesn't measure body fat directly, research has shown that BMI is moderately correlated with more direct measures of body fat obtained from skinfold thickness measurements, bioelectrical impedance, underwater weighing, and other methods.

World Health Organization Categories
