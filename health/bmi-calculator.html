What Is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a widely used measurement to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight relative to their height. It helps categorize individuals as:

[Image of BMI categories chart]
  • Underweight
  • Normal weight
  • Overweight
  • Obese

BMI is calculated using the formula: BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height² (m²). A BMI calculator online performs this calculation instantly, saving you time and effort.
