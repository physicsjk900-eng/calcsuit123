Precision BP Standards

Our BP Risk Audit uses American Heart Association (AHA) guidelines to categorize readings. While "Normal" is historically defined as 120/80, modern clinical focus emphasizes "Elevated" and "Stage 1 Hypertension" as critical early intervention windows for long-term vascular health.
