The Half-Life Concept

Our Caffeine Pulse engine assumes a standard pharmacological half-life of 5 hours. After this duration, approximately 50% of the original dosage remains in the system, potentially disrupting adenosine receptors and sleep cycles indefinitely depending on sensitivity levels.
