What Is an Online Calculator?

An online calculator is a web-based tool that allows users to perform mathematical calculations using the internet. Unlike traditional calculators, these tools work directly in your browser and can be accessed from any device such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, or desktop computers.

A simple calculator online typically supports operations like:

  • Addition & Subtraction
  • Multiplication & Division
  • Percentage & Decimal calculations

Because they are browser-based, free online calculators are accessible anytime and anywhere without downloads or installations.
