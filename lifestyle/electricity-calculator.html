What Is an Electricity Bill Calculator?

An electricity bill calculator estimates the cost of electricity consumption for individual appliances or entire households. It considers factors such as:

  • Power rating of the appliance (Watts)
  • Hours of use per day or month
  • Electricity tariff (cost per kWh)
  • Duration of usage to calculate monthly or yearly costs

With a free electricity bill calculator online, you can determine the energy cost of appliances like refrigerators, air conditioners, heaters, TVs, or lights without complex calculations.
