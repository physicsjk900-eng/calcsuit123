The 24/7 Cost Trap

"Vampire power" or standby power can account for up to 10% of a household's electricity usage. Devices like chargers, TVs, and microwaves consume energy even when turned off if they remain plugged in.

  • Switch to LED bulbs (up to 80% more efficient)
  • Use smart power strips to kill standby power
  • Upgrade to Energy Star® certified appliances
