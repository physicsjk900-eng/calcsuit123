Efficiency Awareness

Vehicle fuel efficiency varies wildly based on driving style, cargo weight, and terrain. Use your car's "lifetime average" MPG or L/100km for the most accurate multi-day trip estimates.

Distance Precision

A 10% error in distance estimation on a long-haul trip can mean the difference between making it to the next station or calling roadside assistance. Always add a 5-10% "buffer" to your calculated distance.

How to Burn Less Fuel

Aerodynamics

Roof racks and open windows at high speeds significantly increase drag and fuel burn.

Weight Management

Every extra 50kg in your vehicle can increase fuel consumption by up to 2%.

Smooth Driving

Rapid acceleration and hard braking are the biggest enemies of fuel efficiency.

The Fuel Formula

Ever wondered how the math works? To get your total cost manually:
