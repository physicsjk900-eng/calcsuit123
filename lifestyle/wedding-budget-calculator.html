The 50/30/20 Wedding Rule

Most wedding planners recommend the 50/30/20 split for a balanced event. This means 50% goes to the venue and catering (the experience), 30% to photography, music, and decor (the atmosphere), and 20% to miscellaneous costs, attire, and gifts.

"A budget isn't about restriction; it's about making sure your money goes to what matters most to you both."

Setup & Teardown Fees
