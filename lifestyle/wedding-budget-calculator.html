What Is a Wedding Budget Calculator?

A wedding budget calculator helps you organize your total wedding budget and estimate how much to allocate to different categories. It considers key expense areas such as:

  • Venue and catering
  • Photography and videography
  • Wedding attire and accessories
  • Decorations and flowers
  • Entertainment and music
  • Miscellaneous expenses

With a free wedding budget calculator online, you can see a percentage-based allocation of your total budget, making it easier to plan and prioritize expenses.
