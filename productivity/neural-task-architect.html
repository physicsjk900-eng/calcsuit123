The Science of Time Management

Modern productivity is no longer about managing hours; it is about managing energy. Research from the University of California suggests that it takes an average of **23 minutes and 15 seconds** to get back into focus after an interruption. Our Neural Task Architect solves this by grouping high-intensity tasks into "Deep Work Blocks" that align with your natural circadian rhythm peaks.

Deep Work

Tasks requiring 100% neural bandwidth. Best performed during biological peaks (approx. 10AM and 4PM for most).

Shallow Work

Administrative tasks, emails, and meetings. Best scheduled for your 'afternoon dip' or early morning warm-up.

Ultradian Recovery

Short 15-20 min breaks after 90 mins of deep focus to allow the brain to switch from focus to diffuse mode.
