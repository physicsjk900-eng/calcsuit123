What Is a Countdown Calculator?

A countdown calculator is a digital tool that calculates the remaining time from the current moment to a specified future target date. It breaks down the countdown into:

  • Remaining Days
  • Remaining Hours
  • Remaining Minutes

It is particularly useful for planning birthdays, tracking project deadlines, and counting down to exams or special occasions.
