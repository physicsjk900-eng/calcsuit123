What Is a Work Hours Calculator?

A work hours calculator is a digital tool that computes the total hours worked by subtracting any break or lunch durations from your shift. It is particularly useful for:

  • Employees tracking daily or weekly hours
  • Freelancers billing clients accurately
  • Managers monitoring team work hours
  • HR professionals calculating payroll

Total Work Hours = End Time – Start Time – Break Duration
